
EUROSOFT HUB FORUMS 



4. Community and Forums – Final Conceptual and Technical Description



4.1 General Purpose of Forums in the Eurosoft Hub

The Eurosoft Hub forums are not social forums, nor do they aim to replicate community-style interaction or typical social network dynamics.

In the Hub, forums play an operational, methodological, and strategic role designed to ensure that organizations —especially those with multiple geographically dispersed units— achieve:

Correct implementation of the Eurosoft Suite

Adoption and standardization of good practices

Process homologation across units

Resolution of methodological and functional questions

Continuous support

Consolidation of applied learning

In other words:

forums are the tool that ensures knowledge is applied, not merely learned in a course, and that it becomes real practices within the organization.



4.2 Types of Forums and Their Function

a) General Role-Based Forums

These forums connect users who perform the same role, regardless of their company or unit.

Purpose:

Align work criteria by role

Resolve common methodological questions

Ensure consistent interpretation

Enable transversal learning

Eurosoft moderates these spaces to ensure methodological coherence.



b) Company and Unit Forums

These are internal spaces where only users from the same company or the same operational unit participate.

Purpose:

Internal system operation

Communicating instructions

Resolving local issues

Coordinating daily progress

Maintaining alignment across units

These forums help all units advance in a homogeneous and coordinated manner.

c) Curated Good Practices Forums

These are the most strategic forums in the Hub.

Only content validated by Eurosoft or by the client’s System Leader is published here, such as:

Solutions successfully applied

Replicable operational practices

Lessons learned

Key guidance for the organization

The purpose is to turn project-generated knowledge into institutional knowledge, available to all business units.

This is one of the key elements that justifies the annual subscription renewal:

continuous access to validated, useful, and applicable content.

4.3 Relationship Between Forums and Gamification

Forums are the core driver of gamification because:

Active participation

Validated answers

Positive mentions

Improvement proposals

Regulated “likes”

Publication of good practices

…all trigger badges, and badges generate points that feed the Motivation Rankings.



Gamification in the Hub does not reward superficial activity. It rewards:

Contributions that help others

Useful knowledge

Correct responses

Continuous improvement

Disciplined participation

Real improvement proposals

4.5 NEW FUNCTIONALITY: Manual Thread Classification

4.5.1 Conceptual Purpose

To prevent forums from becoming disorganized or social spaces—and to preserve their operational and methodological purpose—a structured manual classification system is required.

This classification makes it possible to:

organize knowledge,

accelerate moderation,

enable content-type analysis,

correctly link gamification triggers,

ensure badges are assigned correctly,

and build a searchable, category-based knowledge repository.

Manual classification is essential for ensuring that forums operate as tools for homologation and operational support, not as social environments.

To support these responsibilities, Eurosoft requires an exclusive moderation panel embedded inside the Eurosoft Administration Panel.

This ensures that Eurosoft has a centralized, secure, and privileged interface to:

control all forum activity,

validate contributions,

reclassify threads,

manage gamification triggers,

detect abuse or misuse,

monitor activity per company and per unit,

and preserve the integrity of the operational knowledge.



4.5.2 Functionality (User View)

When creating a new topic, the user must select one of the following thread types:

Duda metodológica

Duda sobre la Suite

Instrucción del cliente

Propuesta de mejora

Buena práctica (pendiente de validación)

Comunicación general

The selected type determines:

how the thread is processed,

gamification triggers,

visibility in dashboards,

follow-up processes,

moderation requirements,

its impact on good practices,

how it feeds KPIs and Rankings.



4.5.3 Manual Validation

Eurosoft and the client’s System Leaders must have access to two formal validation actions:

a) “Mark as helpful answer”

available only to Eurosoft and client leaders

assigns points

assigns the corresponding badge

logs the action

may mark the thread as resolved

b) “Validate good practice”

Once a good practice is approved:

points must be assigned

the corresponding badge must be granted

the thread must be moved/copied to the Good Practices Forum

the validation must be logged as an official validated practice



4.5.4 Technical Requirements

Mandatory tipo_de_hilo field stored as post meta

Quick actions:

validar_respuesta_util()

validar_buena_practica()

reclasificar_tipo_de_hilo()

Full GamiPress integration

Abuse-prevention rules:

no self-likes

maximum likes per user

likes must come from different users

validations restricted to authorized roles

logging and reversible points

Full compatibility with the Eurosoft Moderation Panel



4.6 NEW FUNCTIONALITY: Internal Eurosoft Moderation Panel

4.6.1 Conceptual Purpose

Eurosoft is not a regular user.

Eurosoft’s role is to serve as:

methodological moderator,

validator of good practices,

supervisor of response quality,

guarantor of standardization across units,

technical authority for the methodology,

central pillar of the multi-company model.

To fulfill these responsibilities, Eurosoft requires an exclusive moderation panel that:

centralizes moderation activities,

facilitates validation,

enables thread reclassification,

controls gamification-related actions,

detects abuse,

shows activity per company/unit,

and preserves the integrity of shared knowledge.





4.6.2 Functionality (Eurosoft View)

A dedicated menu must be added:

“Eurosoft Moderation”

This panel must allow:

a) Centralized Thread List

With filters for:

Company

Unit

Author role

Thread type

Status

Date

User

Activity level

b) Quick Actions

Validate helpful answer

Validate good practice

Reclassify thread type

Review likes and suspicious activity patterns

Reverse points when needed

View gamification logs

c) Activity View by Company and Unit

Indicators should include:

number of Duda metodológica threads

number of Duda sobre la Suite threads

validated good practices

improvement proposals

unanswered threads

temporal activity

average response time





4.6.3 Technical Requirements

Custom implementation using WP_List_Table

GamiPress integration for:

point assignment

point reversal

badge triggers

action logging

Strict security:

accessible only to Eurosoft

invisible to all client users

Filtering based on empresa_id and unidad_id

Internal logs for audit and traceability





4.7 Strategic Role of Forums in the Annual Subscription Model

Training has a natural limit.

Once users complete their courses, the ongoing value must come from interaction and applied knowledge.

Annual renewal depends on forums enabling:

continuous support,

real-world problem solving,

standardization across units,

operational alignment,

documentation of good practices,

decision-making based on real conversations.

Thus, forums are the backbone of long-term value in the Eurosoft Hub.



4.8 Conclusion

The combination of forums + classification + validation + gamification + Eurosoft Moderation Panel turns the Hub into a living system of continuous learning and standardization.
